FAQ's

How much is it to use Campus Brainiac?

It is free to download! To can use it and connect with a community of other users who are using it as there. There are features on the app that may require purchases within the app.

Can I connect my Facebook account to the app?

As of right now, you can connect your school account to the app and that is where we will send you a notification on joining the app.*

Will I able to contact my tutor on the app?

Absolutely! Once you find the right tutor for you, that is where you can talk and connect with them.

At any point, can I cancel my scheduled time with my tutor?

Absolutely! If for whatever reason you cannot make your scheduled, you can always message your tutor on the app and cancel your meeting at a later notice.

Can anyone become a tutor?

Yes anyone can become a tutor. If you follow our Brainiac application on the website, it will direct you to the Brainiac Application, where our team will then evaluate you and see whether you are fit to become a Brainiac.

Who can I contact to learn more about Campus Brainiac?

You can submit your question to info@campusbrainiac.com and we will contact you back if you have any questions or concerns with Campus Brainiac.
